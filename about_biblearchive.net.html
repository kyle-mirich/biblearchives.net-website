About BibleArchive.net

Introduction

Welcome to BibleArchive.net, a project started by Kyle Mirich with the mission of creating a digital hub for all Bible-related resources. Whether you're seeking scriptures, study materials, reference tools, or commentaries, we aim to provide them all under one digital roof. Beyond digital consolidation, our community at the forum is growing, fostering discussions and shared learning.

Roadmap

1-Year Goals

  • Expand our community to include members from diverse backgrounds and expertise.
  • Integrate a minimum of 50 new Bible translations into our archive.
  • Launch monthly webinars and workshops to facilitate deeper Bible studies.

3-Year Goals

  • Develop and release a physical hard drive mechanism designed to preserve the sacred text against modern calamities, from technological failures to nuclear fallout scenarios.
  • Triple the amount of study materials and reference tools available.
  • Enhance the forum to include expert-led discussion panels and Q&A sessions.

5-Year Goals

  • Ensure every single digital Bible resource is available for free to all users, irrespective of location or access constraints.
  • Collaborate with global institutions to preserve ancient manuscripts and rare translations digitally.
  • Organize annual global conferences promoting biblical studies and discussions.
